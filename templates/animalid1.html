ARIKOMBAN (NWUPD1 – Elephant)

Arikomban, a wild tusker known to inhabit the Nilambur forest region, was sighted in 2019 during a routine wildlife monitoring patrol. The elephant, known for its calm demeanor, typically roams the forest edges and is rarely seen approaching human settlements. Arikomban plays a key role in maintaining forest ecology by aiding seed dispersal and maintaining natural trails through the dense forest.

Forest officials continue to monitor the movements of Arikomban to ensure both wildlife and human safety, particularly during the harvest season when elephants are known to move closer to villages.
