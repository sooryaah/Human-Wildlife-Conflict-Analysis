Nilambur Forest Region

AVNI (NWUPD3 – Tiger)

ID: TIG01

Year: 2021

Weight Category: ≤ 8,500 pounds

Species: Tiger

Risk Level: Medium

Avni, a female tiger recorded in 2021, has been tracked across the Nilambur and adjoining forest regions. Known for her territorial nature, Avni often roams the deeper forest areas but occasionally approaches agricultural fields in search of prey. While she generally avoids human contact, there have been isolated sightings near settlements.

The forest department has placed Avni under **Medium Risk** observation due to occasional livestock attacks. Motion-triggered cameras and patrol teams help monitor her movements to ensure community safety and prevent further conflict between humans and wildlife.
